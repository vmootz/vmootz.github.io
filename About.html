Mootz, a research startup in the healthy food sector, has identified a niche where popular snack ingredients like potato, squash, and cheese lack recognition for their nutritional value. Despite being associated with cravings, these foods often fall short in providing essential nutrients. To bridge this gap, V-Mootz endeavors to deliver top-tier, nutritious snacks at reasonable prices, catering to health-conscious consumers seeking satisfying alternatives.

One of V-Mootz's key innovations is V-Stickz, a snack specially crafted to address the challenge of children resisting vegetables. By creating this substitute, the company targets parents seeking nutritious options that can entice their kids away from traditional snacks. Furthermore, V-Mootz places a strong emphasis on fortifying their snacks with essential vitamins, recognizing the global prevalence of diseases. By infusing their products with vital nutrients, they aim not only to offer healthier snack options but also to contribute to the overall well-being of consumers amidst health challenges worldwide.



Why lacto-ovo vegetarian?

A lacto-ovo vegetarian diet avoids meat and fish while consuming eggs and dairy products. A lacto-ovo vegetarian diet has some health benefits, including anti-inflammatory properties, blood pressure reduction, diabetes prevention, and weight management.
