1. WHAT WE COLLECT

1.1. Personal information you provide us
We collect personal information you provide us when you use our Consumer Platforms such as your name, address or location, contact number, and email address. If applicable and necessary, we also collect personal information such as your recipient’s name, recipient’s address, recipient’s contact number, recipient’s email address (“Recipient Information”),order details, and other details disclosed as notes/special instructions (“Order Information”).

In relation to Recipient Information, should the recipient be a person other than yourself and you provide the recipient’s personal information when you use our Consumer Platforms, you warrant that you are allowed or have the permission to share with us the recipient’s personal data under your possession, and you agree to be solely responsible for ensuring that such person understands, agrees with, and accepts this Privacy Notice and for protecting the privacy of the recipient’s personal data under your possession. We shall not be liable for any privacy concern arising from your sharing of the Recipient’s Information with us.

1.2. Personal information we are required to collect
We collect personal information as required under any applicable laws, rules, or regulations, including compliance with our legal obligations, such as the name and identification number indicated in the relevant Senior Citizen or Persons with Disability identification card (“Government-Mandated Discount Information”) and a copy of such identification card.

1.3. Personal Information generated automatically
In addition to personal information you provide us, we also collect your personal information generated automatically when you use our Consumer Platforms such as your order history.


2. HOW AND WHY WE COLLECT AND USE

2.1. To fulfill your request
If you choose to use certain features of our Consumer Platforms to avail of our Services, we collect and use your Profile Information to fulfill your request.

2.2. To communicate with you
When you contact us through our Consumer Platforms, we collect and use your Profile Information and any additional personal information you provide to allow us to address your concerns or questions.

2.3. To send you marketing communications and newsletters
You have the option to agree to receive marketing communications from us through SMS, email, social media, messaging application, or other similar means, as well as to subscribe to our newsletters. If you choose to do so, we use your Profile Information you provided us during the registration process to fulfill your request to subscribe to our marketing communications and newsletters. With your consent, we use your Profile Information to send you our marketing communications and newsletters.

2.4. To conduct customer surveys or offers
You have the option to participate in customer surveys or offers. If you choose to participate, we use your Profile Information you provided us during the registration process to fulfill your request to participate in our customer surveys or offers. With your consent, we use your Profile Information and any additional personal information we request, which you voluntarily provide, to contact you for market research, provide you with relevant offers, understand your preferences, and improve our services.

2.5. To protect our vital interests, comply with legal obligations, and as otherwise permitted by law
We may use your personal information to prevent, detect, and fight fraud or other illegal or unauthorized activities, protect our company, customers, brands, and websites, assist in investigations, enforce and defend our rights and assets, comply with laws, regulations, court orders, or other legal obligations, or for any other reasons permitted by law.


3. YOUR CHOICES AND OPTING OUT

3.1. Marketing communications and newsletters
You can choose to stop receiving our marketing communications and newsletters by following the instructions contained in our marketing communications and newsletters or by contacting us. Please refer to 10. How to contact us for information on how to contact us.

Subject to the requirements of applicable laws and regulations, you have the following rights with respect to your personal information we have collected:

4.1. Access and rectification
You can ask us for a copy of the personal information we hold about you or inform us of any changes or corrections to your personal information.

4.2. Erasure, restriction, and objection
In some cases, you can ask us to erase, block, or restrict the processing of the personal information we hold about you or object to particular ways in which we are using your personal information.

4.3. Portability
In some cases, you can ask us to send the personal information you provided us to a third party.

4.4. Withdrawal of consent
Where we are processing your personal information on the basis of your consent, you are entitled to withdraw that consent subject to any applicable law.

4.5. Exercising your rights
Please refer to 7. How to contact us for information on how to exercise these rights.

6. COOKIE POLICY

6.1. Definition
Cookies are small files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. It typically contains information about the website or app itself and a unique identifier that allows the site website or app to recognize your browser or device when you return.

6.2. Purposes
We may use cookies to understand how you use our Consumer Platforms, remember your preferences, track your activity, and personalize your experience. This allows us to improve your experience in using our Consumer Platforms.

6.3. Disabling Cookies
You may disable cookies via your browser settings or by changing your cookie settings in your device. However, if you disable certain cookies, you may not be able to take advantage of the full features of the relevant Platform.
